Chapter 1: Radiant Mornings

After 10 years of a deep, unwavering love, they met for the third time in Rajasthan, where their hearts always seemed to reconnect. As the golden sun dipped behind the desert dunes, she appeared from the left, wearing her favorite yellow dress, the same one that made her glow like a radiant morning. The cool breeze brushed past them, carrying memories of their journey—of laughter, quiet moments, and unspoken promises. In that familiar, serene calm, they didn’t need words. Their love had grown over a decade, and now, standing together in the soft light of Rajasthan’s beauty, it felt as timeless as the desert itself. In her eyes, he found home. And in his presence, she felt peace.
